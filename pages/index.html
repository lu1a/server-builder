Hello world!

Server builder is a cooperative that builds industrial-strength computers here in Finland.
We aren't for profit, we're for the community and the commons.
We build using both first and second hand components.
We sell pre-built, repurposed, and fully second-hand servers.
Please browse our product list to see more.

We are a green organization that breathes life back into second-hand parts so that we can compute a little more sustainably.
Our goal for our first-hand products is to eventually build servers whose parts are only from within the country.
